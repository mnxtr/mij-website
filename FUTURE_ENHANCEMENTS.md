# Future Enhancements

The current implementation (v1.1.0) provides a rock-solid foundation for the MIJ digital presence. Below are the recommended next steps for further elevation.

## Phase 1: Interactive Content & Engagement
- **Interactive Map:** A dynamic SVG or MapBox integration for "Global Footprint" showing offices in Tokyo and Dhaka.
- **Video Hero:** High-definition video portraits for the Recruit and Culture sections.
- **Live News Feed:** Integration with a Headless CMS (Contentful/Sanity) for real-time news management.

## Phase 2: Personalization & UX
- **User Dashboard:** For recurring partners to access specific project resources.
- **Advanced Search:** Algolia-powered AI search for better document discovery.
- **Accessibility Audit:** full WCAG 2.1 compliance certification.

## Phase 3: Technical Performance
- **Image Optimization:** Automated WebP conversion and Cloudinary CDN storage.
- **Analytics:** Integration with GA4 and Hotjar for user behavior heatmaps.
- **SSR/SSG Transition:** Consideration for moving to Next.js if SEO requirements significantly increase.
